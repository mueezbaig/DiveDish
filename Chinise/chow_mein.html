Chow Meins

Chow mein are Chinese stir-fried noodles with vegetables and sometimes meat or tofu. Over the centuries, variations of Chaomian were developed in many regions in China; there are several methods of frying the noodles and range of toppings can be used.

prep steps

9 steps.

cook time

45 min.

serving

10 servings

Tags : South North Italian Chinise Drinks desserts

instructions

step 1

firstly, in a large vessel take water. add 1 tsp salt, 3 tsp oil. get the water to a rolling boil. now add 3 pack noodles and dip completely.

step 2

boil for 3 minutes, or refer to package instructions to know the cooking time. separate the noodles and drain off.

step 3

rinse with cold water to stop cooking and turning noodles mushy.

Chow Mein Sauce

step 1

firstly, in a small bowl take 1 tsp corn flour, 1 tsp chilli powder, ¼ tsp cumin powder and ¼ tsp coriander powder. also add 1 tsp sugar, ¾ tsp salt, 2 tbsp soy sauce and 2 tbsp vinegar.

step 2

further, add 2 tsp oil and ¼ tsp pepper powder. traditionally, sesame oil and oyster sauce is also added. mix well making sure there are no lumps, chowmein sauce ready.

Chow Mein

step 1

firstly, in a large wok, heat 2 tbsp oil and stir fry 3 clove garlic, 1 inch ginger and few spring onion. add ½ onion, ½ green capsicum, ½ red capsicum, ½ carrot and ½ cup cabbage.

step 2

stir fry until the vegetables shrink yet remain crunchy. make sone space in the centre and pour the prepared sauce. stir fry on high flame for a minute until everything is well combined.

step 3

also, add ½ cup cabbage and stir fry. further, add boiled noodles and give a good toss.

step 4

make sure the spices are well combined with noodles. finally, add more spring onions, and enjoy veg chowmein.

ingredients

Water for boiling

Salt 1 tsp

Noodles 3 pack

Corn Flour 1 tsp

Coriander Powder ¼ tsp

Vineager 2 tbsp

Soy Sauce 2 tbsp

Cabbage 1/2 cup sliced
