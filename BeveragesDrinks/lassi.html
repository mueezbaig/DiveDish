Sweet Lassi

Lassi is a blended yogurt drink that is very popular in the Northern Parts of India. It is made with blending curd or yogurt with water or milk, salt or sugar and a few spices or herbs.

prep steps

6 steps.

cook time

10 min.

serving

10 servings

Tags : South North Italian Chinise Drinks desserts

instructions

step 1

Take 2 cups curd (plain yogurt/dahi) in a deep vessel or a pot. We have used the homemade curd in this recipe.

step 2

Add 3 tablespoons sugar and 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder.

step 3

Whisk it using a hand beater or a wired whisk or a mathani or a hand blender until smooth.

step 4

Add 1/2 cup water or milk.

step 5

Whisk again for 1-2 minutes or until a nice frothy layer on top and sugar dissolves completely.

step 6

Sweet Lassi is ready for serving. Pour into two individual serving glasses and garnish with chopped mixed nuts.

ingredients

Plain Curd

Sugar 3 tbsp

Cardamom Powder 1/4 tbsp

Milk 1/2 cup

Assorted Nuts 1 tbsp
