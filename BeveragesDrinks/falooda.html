Falooda

Not quite fit for a bowl but more than a drink, I call falooda a “dessert drink.” It is just not simply a drink that you can sip away because you have to eat some of the edible layers, too. Enjoy this dessert drink with a spoon!

prep steps

9 steps.

cook time

15 min.

serving

10 servings

Tags : South North Italian Chinise Drinks desserts

instructions

step 1

In a tall glass add 2 tbsp rose syrup.

step 2

Further add 2 tbsp soaked sabja seeds.

step 3

Now add 2 tbsp cooked falooda sev.

step 4

Pour 1 cup of chilled milk and stir gently.

step 5

Drop 2 tbsp of gelly without spilling.

step 6

Add in a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

step 7

Further top with 1 tsp of tutti frutti, 1 tsp chopped nuts.

step 8

Drizzle 1 tbsp of rose syrup and top with a cherry.

step 9

Finally, enjoy royal falooda chilled.

ingredients

Sabja seeds 1 cups

Rose Syrup 2 tbsp

Milk 1 L

Ice Cream 1 scoop

Jelly 1 tbsp

Assorted Nuts 1 tbsp
