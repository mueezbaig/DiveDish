Tuna Pasta

We love this Quick and Easy Tuna Pasta recipe - it is cheap, easy, tasty and very quick to make. It has lots of return for little input (just what I like in a recipe!)

prep steps

4 steps.

cook time

25 min.

serving

10 servings

Tags : South North Italian Chinise Drinks desserts

instructions

step 1

Cook the Barilla Penne Rigate No 73 in a saucepan of boiling water following packet directions. Drain, reserving 1/4 cup (60ml) of the cooking liquid.

step 2

Meanwhile, heat half the oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add the onion and chilli. Cook, stirring, for 3 mins. Add the remaining oil and mushroom. Cook for 5 mins or until the mushroom is golden.

step 3

Stir in the Five Brothers Christophe's Summer Vegetable Pasta Sauce, capers and olives. Reduce heat to low. Simmer for 5 mins. Stir in the tuna and chopped parsley. Cook for 2 mins or until tuna is heated through. Season.

step 4

Add the pasta and reserved cooking liquid to the tuna mixture and toss to combine. Divide among serving bowls and top with parsley leaves.

ingredients

Barilla Penne Rigate No 73 500g

Cobram Estate Lemon Infused Australian Extra Virgin Olive Oil 2 tbsp

onion, finely chopped 1

red chilli 1

mushrooms, thinly sliced 200g

Five Brothers Christophe's Summer Vegetable Pasta Sauce 500g

drained Sandhurst Baby Capers in Wine Vinegar 1&1/2 tbsp

drained Sandhurst Pitted Kalamata Olives, halved 75g

Callipo Tuna in Olive Oil, drained 240g

flat-leaf parsley, chopped 1 tbsp
